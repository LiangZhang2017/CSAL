There is a princess, Catalina, who finds the Evening Star beautiful, but she cannot have him because he is a star and she is a human being. One day, she tells the Evening Star to come down and become mortal like she is. The Evening Star goes to his Creator in order to become mortal, but his Creator tells him that human beings are worthless in comparison to the Evening Star. The Evening Star travels back to his place in the sky and sees that the princess was too tired to wait for him and eloped with a young man named Catalin. Catalin is attracted to the princess because her name is Catalina. The princess tells Catalin that he is much better for her than the Evening Star. When she sees the Evening Star back in the sky, she wants him as well. The Evening Star tells the princess that she is nothing but a speck of dust and not a worthy bride for him.
