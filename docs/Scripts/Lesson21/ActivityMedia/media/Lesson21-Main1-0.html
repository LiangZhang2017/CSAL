Effects of Smoking
Many Americans smoke but few truly know the damage that is caused by smoking. Smoking harms nearly every organ in your body and is hurts your health. Millions of Americans have health problems that are caused by smoking. Smoking is a leading cause of cancer and death from cancer. Smoking causes cancer of the lungs, throat, kidney, mouth, stomach, and many more organs. Smoking also causes heart disease and stroke. Smoking is the leading cause of premature, preventable death in the country. You are never too old to smoking, regardless of age, quitting can help reduce the risk of heart disease, stroke, and many cancers listed above. The best way to prevent smoking related illnesses is to stop smoking all together. If you are struggling with quitting smoking, contact a doctor to receive help.
