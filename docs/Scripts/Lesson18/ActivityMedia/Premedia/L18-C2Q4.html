Why eat out when it is easy to make a quesadilla at home? First, gather your ingredients. You can use one cup of sliced steak, diced chicken, or shrimp. You will also need 1 cup of shredded cheese. Next, place a flour tortilla in a frying pan and spread half of the meat and cheese evenly over half of the tortilla. Then fold the tortilla in half. Last, turn the quesadilla over after a few minutes and cook the other side. Finally cut the quesadilla into wedges and serve with sour cream, salsa, or guacamole.
